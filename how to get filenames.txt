How to get a list of filenames
*note this might not work with non-tue laptops, in that case contact me(Niels)


step 1: get all your json files unpacked and put into a single folder.

step 2: hold shift and right-click on the folder containing your jsons.

step 3: in the menu select: "Open PowerShell window here"

step 4: a blue window should appear, in this window paste the code below and press ctrl+enter

cmd /r dir /d > filenames.txt
